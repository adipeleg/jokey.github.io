1. Our Commitment

Jokey is a humor-focused social app intended for a general adult audience. We have zero tolerance for any form of child sexual abuse or exploitation (CSAE) and strictly prohibit any content, conduct, or activity that harms or endangers children.

2. Scope of this Policy

This Policy applies to:

  • All content posted, uploaded, shared, or transmitted through the App, including jokes, text, images, videos, and profile information.
  • All user behavior, including messaging, commenting, or any other interaction with other users.
  • All users of the App, regardless of location.

3. Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate action, including content removal and account termination:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM): Any visual, written, or audio content that depicts or describes sexually explicit activity involving a minor (anyone under the age of 18).
  • Sexualized Content Involving Minors: Any jokes, descriptions, comments, role-play, or fantasies that sexualize minors, even if intended as ‚Äúhumor‚Äù.
  • Exploitation or Trafficking: Any content that promotes, trivializes, or depicts the sexual exploitation, trafficking, or abuse of minors.
  • Links or References to CSAE Material: Any links, hints, coded references, or instructions that point to CSAM or CSAE-related material on or off the platform.

4. Prohibited Behavior

The following behavior is strictly prohibited on Jokey:

  • Grooming or Solicitation: Attempting to establish a relationship with a minor in order to exploit them, including building trust, manipulating, or coercing them.
  • Requesting Sexual Content from Minors: Asking, encouraging, or pressuring minors to share sexual or intimate images, videos, or information.
  • Targeting Minors: Contacting or targeting minors for romantic, sexual, or exploitative purposes.
  • Harassment or Intimidation of Minors: Any form of bullying, threats, or harassment directed at minors.

5. Age Restrictions

Jokey is not directed toward children. The App is intended for users aged 13 and above, and may be restricted to older users depending on regional requirements and the nature of user-generated content.

We do not knowingly allow or encourage children to use the App. If we become aware that a user is under the minimum allowed age, we may take steps to restrict or terminate the account in accordance with applicable law.

6. Reporting and User Tools

We encourage users to help keep Jokey safe. If you encounter any content or behavior that may involve CSAE or otherwise place children at risk, you should report it immediately.

You can report concerns or violations by contacting us at: tripaimailer@gmail.com.

Where available, in-app reporting tools may also be used to flag content or users for review.

7. Enforcement and Actions

When we become aware of potential CSAE content or behavior, we may take one or more of the following actions:

  • Immediate removal of violating content.
  • Temporary or permanent suspension or termination of user accounts.
  • Restriction of features or access to the App.
  • Preservation of data as required for investigations.
  • Notification and cooperation with law enforcement or child protection authorities, where appropriate.

8. Cooperation with Authorities

We may report suspected CSAE content or behavior to appropriate law enforcement agencies or child protection organizations, and we cooperate with valid legal requests and investigations.

This may include sharing account information, content, and usage data as permitted or required by applicable law.

9. Education and Prevention

We are committed to improving our policies and practices over time. We periodically review our safety measures, reporting processes, and enforcement actions to better prevent and respond to CSAE-related risks.
